Target C++20 with GCC (`g++ -O3 -march=native -std=c++20`). The
program will be invoked as `./prog <N>` and may read stdin via
`std::cin` or `read(0, ...)`. Prefer raw arrays, `std::vector`, and
`std::array` over higher-level abstractions; avoid `std::endl`
(which flushes) in favor of `'\n'`; call
`std::ios_base::sync_with_stdio(false);` and `std::cin.tie(nullptr);`
at program start. Use `<cstdio>` for output when formatting is
simple. Do not depend on any third-party library beyond the C++
standard library.
